About Me

I am now an Assistant Professor at the School of Software Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University, China. Prior to joining Sun Yat-sen University, I was a Postdoc at Zhejiang Univeristy advised by Prof. Shanping Li and Dr. Xin Xia. I got my Ph.D degree in December 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Keqing He from Wuhan University, China.

My currently research focuses on mining knowledge from rich Software Engineering data and developing knowledge-based intelligient approaches to improving developers' productivity and software quality. In terms of the techniques, I am interested in adopting different data analytics techniques, including data mining, knowledge graph, machine learning and program analysis.
